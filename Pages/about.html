About Us

Who We Are

This 40 year old establishment famous for its poutines and burgers was handed into the able hands of Chef Roger in 2023 to build upon and grow. Chef Roger worked at the famous restaurants in Banff and Jasper, Alberta for 10 years where he served/cooked his steaks for the guests including royalty. Chef Roger introduced the now famous Classic Jacks burger made in house with premium beef and mild seasonings which epitomes the ultimate juicy burger experience and hence its rightly called a classic! Ming shared her secret Poutine recipe to the Chef who augmented it with newer flavors as the butter chicken poutines and Jallaf fries.
